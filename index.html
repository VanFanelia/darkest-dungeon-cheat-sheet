Necromancer Lord · Ruins
What it does
  • Moves backward each turn; summons Skeletons into rank 1 every round
  • Higher tiers: Skeletons spawn with Stealth for 2 rounds
Strategy
  • Kill Skeletons immediately (Crusader in front); stack Blight on the Necromancer
  • Use pull skills to keep him in melee range (Houndmaster, Bounty Hunter)
Recommended
The Prophet · Ruins
What it does
  • Starts rank 4; marks 1–2 ranks → "Rubble of Ruin" hits those ranks (potentially lethal)
  • 3 Pews block front ranks; only 1 normal action/turn
Strategy
  • Stack debuffs (Intimidate, Weakening Curse) to reduce Rubble damage to near zero
  • Man-at-Arms Guard protects marked heroes; Shieldbreaker Serpent Sway dodges it
Recommended
The Hag · Weald
What it does
  • Throws a hero into the Cauldron (can't act, takes damage every action)
  • 2 actions/round → DOTs tick twice; high Stun resist
Strategy
  • Ignore the pot — focus all damage on the Hag directly
  • Grave Robber Shadow Fade = untargetable; stack Bleed on Hag
Recommended
Avoid
Brigand Pounder · Weald
What it does
  • Cannon has extreme PROT; Fusilier fires it at round end → massive AoE damage
  • Cannon Ripostes non-lethal hits against it
Strategy
  • Kill/stun the Fusilier (Lighter) BEFORE he acts every round
  • Crits bypass the Cannon's PROT; ignore other Brigands
Recommended
Swine Prince + Wilbur · Warrens
What it does
  • Wilbur marks heroes → Prince hits marked targets for devastating damage
  • Hitting Wilbur = Party-wide Rage! Killing Wilbur = Permanent Berserk!
Strategy
  • NEVER deal damage to Wilbur; only 0-damage stuns allowed on him
  • Arbalest Rallying Flare removes all marks → Prince uses weak Wild Flailing
Recommended
The Flesh · Warrens
What it does
  • 4 body parts (Head, Bone, Heart, Butt) share one HP pool, all act each round
  • Bone: AoE Stun; Head: heavy Bleed rank 1–2; Butt: Blight rank 3–4; Heart: heals others
Strategy
  • Target the Heart first — it has 0% PROT and heals the other parts
  • DoTs bypass all part PROT; Shieldbreaker Pierce ignores PROT on other parts
Recommended
The Siren · Cove
What it does
  • Charms a random hero (fights for her); 2 actions/round; summons Pelagics
  • High Bleed resist; apply Holy Water before the fight to resist charm
Strategy
  • Equip Holy Water on ALL 4 heroes BEFORE the fight (big charm resist bonus)
  • Damage race — kill her fast; stack Blight; Leper resists charm with Solemnity
Recommended
Drowned Crew · Cove
What it does
  • Summons Anchorman (anchors frontmost hero — immobile + Crew heals)
  • 3 actions/round → DoTs extremely effective; low Stun resist
Strategy
  • Kill the Anchorman immediately when it appears (stops healing)
  • Stack Blight — 3 actions/round means DoTs deal massive total damage
Recommended
Brigand Vvulf · Town Event
What it does
  • Places a bomb on a random rank each round — explodes at round end
  • Barrel Ripostes; calls Brigand reinforcements; needs Lvl 5 heroes!
Strategy
  • Man-at-Arms MUST Guard the bomb target every round (essential)
  • Stack Bleed/Blight — his many actions make DOTs extremely effective
Recommended
The Shrieker · Town Event
What it does
  • Steals 8 trinkets; fight lasts exactly 4 rounds — auto-win after round 4!
  • Fights with its Nest; extreme DODGE (40–60%); immune to Blight
Strategy
  • Just survive 4 rounds — no need to deal damage at all
  • Kill the Nest for bonus loot; Highwayman Riposte triggers constantly on its attacks
Recommended
The Shambler · Any Dungeon
What it does
  • Always fights at 0 torch (surprise round!); constantly shuffles party
  • Summons 2 Tentacles/round — Tentacles gain buffs with every action
Strategy
  • Kill Tentacles IMMEDIATELY each round before they become unkillable
  • Use heroes who attack from any rank; bring a stress healer for darkness
Recommended
Avoid
Shuffling Horror · DD Q1
What it does
  • 2 actions/round; constantly shuffles the party; high stress damage
  • Cultist Priest summons reinforcements if killed
Strategy
  • STUN the Cultist Priest — do NOT kill him!
  • Riposte builds work well; use heroes who can attack from any rank
Recommended
Templar Impaler · DD Q2
What it does
  • "Revelation": 40 Stress + damage to ALL (Talisman of the Flame negates this!)
  • Heavy Blight melee in front; low Move resist — can be pushed back
Strategy
  • 3× Talisman of the Flame REQUIRED (Revelation: 40→8 Stress, 0 dmg)
  • Guard the unprotected 4th hero; push Impaler backward; bring Antivenom
Recommended
Templar Warlord · DD Q2
What it does
  • Like Impaler: "Revelation" = Talisman of the Flame required; AoE Blight attacks
  • Cannot be repositioned (98% Move resist)
Strategy
  • 3× Talisman of the Flame REQUIRED; Guard the unprotected hero
  • Houndmaster Cry Havoc for stress heal between rooms; stock Antivenom
Recommended
Mammoth Cyst · DD Q3
What it does
  • 2 actions/round; White Cell Stalk teleports party to random room on the map
  • At 50% HP the Cyst begins self-healing; Stalk respawns when killed
Strategy
  • Stun or kill the Stalk every round before it acts — teleport is the main threat
  • Blight effective; Plague Doctor Blinding Gas stuns both Cyst and Stalk at once
Recommended
Heart of Darkness · DD Q4
What it does
  • Phase 1: Only killing Imperfect Reflections damages the Ancestor (kill 5 to advance)
  • Phase 4: "Come Unto Your Maker" instantly kills one hero at 2/3 HP AND again at 1/3 HP — no retreat possible!
Strategy
  • Phase 4: Maximize burst damage to skip HP thresholds, OR prepare self-sustaining heroes for the final 2-hero phase
  • Self-healing is critical — party shrinks to 2; no retreat from Q4!
Recommended
